# Introduction

##### Tools for collective Imagination

---

Our mission is to make creating on the internet free and valuable by building products for people to be more supported and valued for their creativity.

Build your project using the Zora Protocol and plug into Zora's distribution and business model from day one.
We've created a series of tools that makes it easy to get started building.

#### Our Stack

- [Contracts:](../docs/smart-contracts/creator-tools/intro) Deploy contracts for an NFT project

- [API:](../docs/zora-api/intro) Retrieve NFT metadata and market data on Zora NFTs

- [Zora Network](../docs/zora-network/intro) A Layer 2 designed for putting media onchain
